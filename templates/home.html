Generating novel chapters will take around 1-2 minutes per 100 chapters (unless they have already been uploaded), then you will get a pop-up on your screen saying that it has completed it. There is no initial warning to tell you that the script has started running.

Generating chapters will create a webpage with the content of the given chapter. And will be uploaded in the Chapters button above.

This tool can update novels fast and with quality.

Any novel from lightnovelpub.vip can be scraped and uploaded.
